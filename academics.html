6.454 Graduate Seminars in Area I (Fall 2011)

I presented a few papers on the bounds on reliable boolean function computation with noisy boolean gates:
  • R. L. Dobrushin and S. I. Ortyukov, Lower bound for the redundancy of self-correcting arrangements of unreliable functional elements , Prob. Inf. Transm., vol. 13, pp. 59-65, 1977.
  • N. Pippenger, On networks of noisy gates, Proc. 26th IEEE Symp. Foundations Comput. Sci., 1985, pp. 30-36.
  • P. Gacs and A. Gal, Lower bounds for the complexity of reliable boolean circuits with noisy gates, IEEE Trans. Inform. Th., Vol. 40, No. 2, Mar. 1994.
  • Presentation slides (PDF)

6.897 Seminars on Cloud Computing (Spring 2011)

This is a reading course on cloud computing systems and I presented the following paper:
  • A. Ghodsi, M. Zaharia, B. Hindman, A. Konwinski, S. Shenker, and I. Stoica, Dominant resource fairness: Fair allocation of heterogeneous resources in datacenters, Proceedings of the 8th USENIX conference on Networked systems design and implementation (NSDI'11)
    • Presentation slides (PDF)
