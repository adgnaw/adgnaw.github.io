About me

I obtained my Ph.D in June 2014 and am now working at Two Sigma Investments as a researcher.

I did my graduate studies at MIT from 2008 to 2014. I was a member of Professor Gregory Wornell's Signals, Information and Algorithms laboratory in the Research Laboratory of Electronics (RLE). You can read more about my research here. I was featured in the April 2013 RLE Student Spotlight.

I obtained my Bachelor of Applied Sciences (major in electrical engineering + minor in mathematics) from the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at the University of Toronto.
